About Lightspeed

Lightspeed is a multi-channel, high speed astronomical imager for the Magellan Clay telescope that is currently under construction.  Using cutting-edge CMOS image sensors that achieve sub-electron read noise, Lightspeed will allow for high-precision studies of the fastest optically variable sources in the sky, such as optical pulsars, flaring accretion disks, and small solar system objects occulting background stars.

In September 2025, proto-Lightspeed, a single-channel prototype for Lightspeed, was commissioned on the Magellan Clay telescope.
